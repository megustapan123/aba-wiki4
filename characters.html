Character Rosters

Anime Battle Arena groups its fighters into rosters by the anime or manga series they come from, making it easy to find every version of your favorite character. The current build spans 197 characters across dozens of series. Below are highlights from the most popular rosters — head to the official wiki for the complete, always up-to-date list.
